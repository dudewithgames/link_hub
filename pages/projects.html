"There is a mathematical beauty to every geometric shape. For the cubes, pyramids, and spheres of Geometria, this is a simple truth they have long been content with. One day, however, a sphere realizes a grave injustice: all the other shapes have multiple faces and he only has one. To rectify this, he decides to steal the third dimension. This causes all the other shapes to be reduced to two dimensions and one face. With the ability to switch between planes, a simple square is tasked with restoring the third dimension."
